Brevet de Technicien Supérieur en Services Informatiques aux Organisations

About

The Brevet de Technicien Supérieur en Services Informatiques aux Organisations (BTS SIO) is a two-year training program that takes place in French high schools, private schools, or professional training centers. It can be pursued as part of continuing education, apprenticeship, distance learning with the CNED, or initial training.

BTS SIO offers two distinct specialties: BTS SIO Option SISR and BTS SIO Option SLAM.

The BTS SIO student acquires skills in three areas of activity, two of which are common to both specialties:
  • Support and provision of IT services
  • Cybersecurity of IT services.

    Specialization

    Option SISR

    Systems and Network Administration:

    The holder of this specialization participates in the administration of systems and networks. They can modify or adjust infrastructure solutions and the quality of service of network equipment. They acquire the skills to design, install, test, and implement a network infrastructure solution. The student also learns to administer and supervise this network.

    Option SLAM

    Application Development and Design:

    The student participates in the design, development, implementation, and maintenance of the software components of the application solution. They acquire skills in the design and development of an application solution, its maintenance, as well as databases and digital data management.
